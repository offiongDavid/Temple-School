A Message from
the Director

Founding Director

Mrs Shirley Okharedia

“

At Temple School, we believe that education is far more than the simple transmission of knowledge; it is the deliberate cultivation of curiosity, discipline, and excellence in every child entrusted to our care.

For over two decades, our institution has remained steadfast in its commitment to providing a premier co-educational experience. From the nurturing foundational years at our Ilupeju campus to the advanced learning environments in Ikeja, we guide our students through a carefully structured journey designed to unlock their full potential.

Our Educational Philosophy

We recognize that every child is unique. Our approach is built upon three core pillars:

  • ◆ Personalized Learning: Tailoring our teaching to meet individual student needs.
  • ◆ Rigorous Assessment: Utilizing continuous feedback to ensure consistent progress.
  • ◆ Global Curriculum: Aligning our academics with international standards to ensure our learners thrive in an ever-evolving world.

Beyond the Classroom

Our mission extends well beyond academic markers. We are dedicated to raising confident, morally grounded, and forward-thinking individuals who are prepared to contribute meaningfully to global society.

At Temple School, the learning journey transcends the classroom walls. Through diverse enrichment programs, collaborative projects, and robust community engagement, our students develop the essential life skills required for leadership and lifelong success.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our vision remains steadfast: to uphold a proud tradition of excellence while embracing the innovations of the future. We ensure that every child who walks through our doors leaves equipped, empowered, and inspired.

Together, we are not just educating students; we are shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Mrs Shirley Okharedia

Founding Director
