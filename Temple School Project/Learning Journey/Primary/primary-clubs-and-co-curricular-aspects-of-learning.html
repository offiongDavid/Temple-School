A wide range of co-curricular activities is available for Temple children. Through enrichment opportunities beyond the classroom, pupils develop creativity, confidence, teamwork, discipline and leadership skills.

Weekly Enrichment Programme

Every child is encouraged to participate in weekly clubs and activities. These programmes help pupils discover passions, improve practical skills and become more confident learners.
