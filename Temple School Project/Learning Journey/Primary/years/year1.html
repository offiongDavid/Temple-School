Year 1 Learning Journey

Year One Experience

Learning Through Play

Smiles, fun and learning are the defining elements of Year One at Temple School.

Learners in this stage are typically 5 years old, with the year group consisting of 5 arms and an average of 15 children per class.

Classroom Environment

A Safe & Inspiring Environment

Happy Learning

Bright colours, beautiful displays, and stimulating visuals create enthusiasm and excitement for learning.

Modern Classrooms

Flexible furniture, comfortable writing spaces, visual stimulators, and 21st-century learning aids.

Close Supervision

Every class is supervised by both a class teacher and assistant teacher, ensuring pupils are never left unattended.

Our curriculum blends the British Key Stage One curriculum with the Nigerian Primary One curriculum to create a wholesome and robust educational experience.

Assemblies

Year One assemblies are exciting, creative, and carefully planned to combine fun, physical activity, intellectual engagement, and important life lessons.

Parental Involvement

We maintain continuous communication with parents through emails, phone calls, and face-to-face interactions to ensure a strong partnership in child development.

Co-Curricular Experiences

Year One learners enjoy practical learning through carefully planned field trips and excursions.

These experiences help children connect classroom theory with real-world learning while preparing them for future Years 2–6 activities.
