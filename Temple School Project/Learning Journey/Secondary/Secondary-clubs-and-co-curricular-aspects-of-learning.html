Secondary Clubs & Co-Curricular Activities

Clubs & Co-Curricular Activities

Temple Secondary School offers a wide range of co-curricular activities designed to develop confidence, creativity, discipline and leadership. Every student is encouraged to participate, explore interests and grow beyond the classroom.

Weekly Enrichment Opportunities

Meetings are held weekly, giving students structured opportunities to build practical life skills, collaborate with peers and discover passions in arts, academics, sports and leadership.
