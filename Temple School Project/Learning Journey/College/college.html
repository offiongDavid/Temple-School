Academic Pathways

Temple College provides structured academic pathways designed to equip students with the knowledge, confidence and global competencies required for university admission and future professional success. Through personalised guidance, rigorous instruction and career-focused support, students are empowered to excel beyond the classroom.

Temple College partners with Braemar College to offer students access to the Ontario Secondary School Diploma Programme (OSSD), providing a globally recognised academic route into leading universities across Canada and beyond.

This pathway equips students with strong academic foundations, flexibility in subject combinations and personalised university guidance for future success.

The Cambridge Advanced Level exam is an international school leaving qualification regulated by the British Council.

We deliver the traditional 2-year programme: Advanced Subsidiary (AS) and Advanced Level (A2) paying individual attention to students, engaging in smaller group sizes and providing a nurturing environment.

Within this framework, we offer a variety of subject choices coupled with well-equipped science and computer laboratories to meet the demands of advanced study.

Students usually take four subjects in the first year and focus on three (3) subjects for the A2 level.
