The Temple School Advantage: A Legacy of Excellence Across Lagos

Temple School is more than just an educational institution; it is a premier network of academic excellence strategically positioned across the heart of Lagos. Operating from four distinct, state-of-the-art campuses located in Ilupeju, Ikeja, and Allen Avenue. we offer a seamless, all-inclusive educational journey designed to empower students from their first steps through to young adulthood.

Experience the Temple School difference where every campus is a gateway to a brighter future.

Early Years & Primary (Ilupeju): Nestled in a quiet residential environment, our foundational campuses nurture curiosity, emotional intelligence, and strong literacy and numeracy skills essential for lifelong success.

Secondary & College (Ikeja & Allen Avenue): Our advanced campuses serve as hubs of innovation, focusing on creative problem-solving, collaboration, and academic excellence that prepares students for the global stage.

Why Choose Temple School?

We provide a consistent gold standard of education across all locations, making Temple School a trusted choice for parents seeking quality, structure, and excellence.
