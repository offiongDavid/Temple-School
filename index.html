The Temple School Advantage: A Legacy of Excellence Across Lagos

Temple School is more than just an educational institution; it is a premier network of academic excellence strategically positioned across the heart of Lagos.

Operating from four distinct, state-of-the-art campuses located in Ilupeju, Ikeja, and Allen Avenue, we offer a seamless and all-inclusive educational journey designed to empower students from their very first steps through to young adulthood.

Early Years & Primary

Nestled in a calm residential environment, our Ilupeju campuses nurture curiosity, emotional intelligence, literacy, and numeracy skills.

Secondary & College

Our Ikeja & Allen Avenue campuses focus on innovation, leadership, collaboration, and academic excellence for the global stage.

Experience the Temple School difference where every campus becomes a gateway to a brighter future.
